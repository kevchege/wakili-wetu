Section 5 – Prohibition of Discrimination

No employer shall discriminate directly or indirectly against an employee on grounds of race, colour, sex, religion, political opinion, nationality, ethnic origin, disability, pregnancy, or HIV status.

Section 35 – Termination Notice

A contract of service not being a contract to perform specific work shall be terminable by either party by giving notice.

Section 41 – Notification and Hearing before Termination

An employer shall explain to the employee the reason for termination and hear any representations before dismissal.
Failure to follow due process makes termination unfair.
