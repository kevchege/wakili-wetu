Section 24 – Interest in Land

The registration of a person as proprietor of land shall vest in that person absolute ownership together with all rights and privileges.

Section 26 – Certificate of Title

A certificate of title issued by the Registrar shall be taken as prima facie evidence that the person named is the absolute owner.

Section 80 – Rectification by Court

The court may order rectification of the register if it is satisfied that registration was obtained through fraud or mistake.
